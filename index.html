About

I work in specialist and leadership recruitment, with experience spanning IT roles through to executive search mandates. Since 2019, I have participated in national and international searches, supporting clients in securing both critical technical talent and senior leadership appointments.

My background began in IT recruitment, focusing on direct search for complex and hard-to-fill roles. Over time, I became involved in leadership hiring, contributing to board-facing shortlists and supporting the defense of candidate recommendations. I have also worked as lead recruiter on a key international account, coordinating closely with a colleague and serving as a main point of contact for the client.

I take a candidate-first, evidence-based approach. Each search is tailored to the role’s strategic requirements and day-to-day realities. Clear communication, structured search methodology, and market insight guide my process, ensuring sustainable outcomes for both clients and candidates.
